Why this website?

About

Since years I have been working in the seismic industry and writing code. I love to work with open source tools and learn via code from others.

The Website

The initial idea was to write down some lab notes for my personal use and stash them in an ever-accessible place so I could retrieve them when necessary. The markdown format was perfect to take notes while taking advantage of equation and image rendering. I then discovered on the Jekyll engine to generate blogs easily and on the beautiful and amazingly well documented theme Feeling Responsive. I only had to adapt the theme to my content.
